I work in the field of marketing and sales, shifting to Front End field is completely due to my personal interests and feelings, this work makes me really happy. Right now I am taking my parental leave (Elternzeit) and working as a freelancer. My daily job is to further Front End Knowledge, and build websites for friends and individuals.

I graduated from TU Munich(Technische Universität München) in 2013 with a Master degree. My master thesis was focusing on the software analysis and research in the field of industrial products.

I have around 10 months experiences with Forex Coding, which is to code based on mathmatic rules and personal understandings for the machine, in order to trade Forex 7/24 without stop.

Since 07/2018 I started to focus on the Front End development, and really enjoy the work, it rocks! Right now I am enrolled in the Microsoft Professional Program for Front-End Web Development. I will finish it around April this year.

My personal goal is to become a professional in Full Stack mainly with Javascript, HTML and CSS in the next few years.
